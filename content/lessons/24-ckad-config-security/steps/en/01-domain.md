## Application Environment, Configuration and Security

This public CKAD domain is weighted at **25%**, the largest share. It mixes
configuration, resource governance, identity, authorization, admission behavior,
and Pod security.

You will practice:

- ConfigMaps and Secrets
- requests, limits, ResourceQuota and LimitRange
- ServiceAccounts, Roles and RoleBindings
- SecurityContext and Linux capabilities
- creating and using a simple CRD

Use namespace **`ckad-sec`**.
