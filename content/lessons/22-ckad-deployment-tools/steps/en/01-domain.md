## Application Deployment

This public CKAD domain is weighted at **20%**. It focuses on the ways you ship
or change an application after the Pod shape is known.

You will practice:

- rolling updates on Deployments
- blue/green switching with selectors
- canary release by replica ratio
- installing an existing local Helm chart
- applying a Kustomize overlay

Use namespace **`ckad-deploy`** for every task.
